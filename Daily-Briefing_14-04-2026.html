📚 30-Minute Learning Focus

Today: Drone & Regs — UK CAA Rules for Commercial Flight

Why this matters right now: Every drone job you apply for will ask about your CAA certifications. Understanding the Open Category (A1/A2/A3), the A2 Certificate of Competency, and when you need Specific Category authorisation is the foundation of every commercial drone career in the UK.
📖 Free Resource: CAA Drone Guidance for Operators — start with the "Flying commercially" section.
🎓 FlyCee A2 CofC course — affordable online prep for the CAA A2 exam (~£30).
🎯 Your 30-min exercise:

1. Open the CAA drone website and read the Open Category overview (10 min).
2. Draw or type out the A1/A2/A3 subcategory rules on a single page — what drone classes they allow, max height, distance from people (10 min).
3. Write down ONE real job from today's listings where this knowledge is the key requirement and what you'd say in a cover letter about your drone experience (10 min).

Save your notes in a "Drone Regs Cheat Sheet" document — you'll use this in interviews.
