Example: “Crypto blog post (800 words): £80, delivered in 48 hours.” Having a rate card means you can respond to any inbound inquiry immediately with a professional quote, not an estimate. The skill you learn: productising your services — the difference between a freelancer who quotes every job from scratch and one who has a scalable, repeatable business model. This document becomes your Fiverr gig description, your Upwork service listing, and your client pitch email.
